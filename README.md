# Lomilab

🔥 Lomilab is a platform that optimizes the use of AI/ML models and datasets, providing powerful tools to deploy and visualize models with just a few lines of code. With Lomilab, integrating AI into your application becomes easier than ever, whether you are a beginner, an expert, or someone not specialized in AI/ML, breaking down barriers and bringing AI/ML closer to everyone.

## Purpose
#### Background
- AI and machine learning have achieved significant breakthroughs, but accessing and deploying complex models is still limited due to factors such as:
    - Difficult-to-access technology infrastructure.
    - Deep knowledge of machine learning models is a major barrier for beginners.
    - Lack of standardization in the use of datasets and models.
    - Deployed models are not visualized, making it difficult for newcomers.

#### Objectives
Lomilab was created with the goal of:
- Making the use and experience of AI easy and not too demanding for users.
- Simplifying model deployment and dataset analysis.
- Promoting creativity by bringing models/datasets to the cloud to foster AI/ML development.
- Building a community.

## Who is Lomilab for?
- AI/ML/Data researchers: Easily experiment with new ideas with user-friendly visualized models and data.
- Application developers: Quickly integrate advanced AI models into applications.
- AI beginners: Provide documentation, intuitive tools, and resource libraries to get started.
- Enterprises: Simplify large-scale AI deployment with cloud integration tools.

## Key Features
    Simple Deployment:
- Easily use AI/ML models deployed from available libraries.
- Download, update, and use with just a few simple commands.
- Data is continuously saved when updated.
- Supports cloud hosting both offline, online, and hybrid.

🚀 Automated and Visualized Training Process
- Supports distributed training on multiple GPUs/CPUs.
- Provides user-friendly charts and logs model parameters.
- Supports 3D visualization for neural networks for each chart and dataset.

🛠 No Installation Worries
- Supports GPU/CPU during data training and analysis.
- Supports popular AI/ML frameworks on the market.
- API with user-friendly commands.

🌐 Cloud Model Deployment
- Integrates with cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, etc.
- Deploy APIs in just a few seconds.

🔗 Developer Community
- Can establish a community or organization to share different datasets or AI models for development.
